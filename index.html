About Me

In 2014, I earned a Master of Public Health degree from the University of Michigan School of Public Health. Through the program, I developed skills relevant to my desire to promote sexual and reproductive health and drive to have a measurable impact among those unable to access health care. Now, I possess competency in program development, planning, implementation, and evaluation, as well as in research methods and health communication.


Over the past seven years, I have gained public health experience in research, evaluation, and project management throughout six states (AL, GA, MI, MS, NJ, TX) and four countries (Kenya, Tanzania, U.S., Zambia).


To better serve and reach youth with public health interventions, I am currently pursuing a Full Stack Flex Web Development certificate.
